Full new stock research for $ARGUMENTS.

Workflow:
1. One-paragraph business summary
2. Full GARP checklist (8 dimensions, scored 1–5 with rationale)
3. Bear/base/bull scenarios to 2031 (same format as /scenarios)
4. Where it fits (or doesn't) in my existing themes
5. Key risks — what makes this thesis wrong
6. Valuation sanity check — what am I paying, what growth is implied
7. Recommendation: research further or pass, with clear reasoning
8. If I say "add to watchlist", create a new entry in portfolio_state.json
   with weight: 0 and all data populated

Output format:

### Business Summary
[One paragraph]

### GARP Checklist
[Full 8-dimension table, same as /checklist format]

### Scenarios to 2031
[Full scenario table, same as /scenarios format — revenue, margin, net income, EPS, total return, IRR]

### Portfolio Fit
[How it complements or overlaps with existing holdings and themes]

### Key Risks
[Bullet list — what specifically breaks the thesis]

### Valuation Sanity Check
[P/E, EV/EBITDA, P/FCF, PEG vs peers. What growth rate is priced in?]

### Recommendation
[Research further / Pass — direct verdict with GARP reasoning]
